PREMIUM WINDOWS & DOORS WEBSITE

SMALL BUSINESS WORDPRESS-BASED WEBSITE

PREMIUM Windows & Doors is a Chicago area family-owned window and door replacement company that offers services, including window, patio, and entry door installation and replacement.

The WordPress-based website for this company is developed in a clear and professional style with a sharp-cut interface that makes navigation easier. Subtle animations on the home page additionally complement the experience of the landing page and the beautifully designed slider in the hero section provides just enough information about the page you are currently on.

The website has a dedicated image gallery section which can be interesting for potential clients of the company as it contains previous works of PREMIUM Windows & Doors, and when they decide to get in touch, they have a contact form or call-now button (which by the way automatically starts the call on handheld devices) on their disposal.

Do they decide to schedule an appointment, there is a whole section on the website just for that, and the visitors can use this form to securely and comfortably make an appointment or schedule an interview.

The website is entirely based on WordPress Content Management System with a couple of additional plugins, and the design, responsiveness, and animations are done by writing custom CSS code inside of the website theme.
